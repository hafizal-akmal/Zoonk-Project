ABOUT ZOONKIES!

ZOONKIES (pronounced /zuː/n/kiːz/)

Meet The Zoonkies! the most adorable a cappella group ever assembled, where every member sings in a different dialect of devotion! Our group is an imaginary vocal group with 5 member, taken from the concept of 5 love language where each of them represents the manifestations of this concept. Together, they don't just sing—they perform a symphony of the heart.
